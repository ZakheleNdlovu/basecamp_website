About Basecamp+

About

Basecamp+ is a small start-up located in Chief Albert Luthuli, Benoni. It aims to provide a comprehensive suite of digital and administrative services. The business focuses on being a solution hub for students, job seekers, entrepreneurs and small businesses. Our core services include standard offerings like photocopying, printing and scanning, complemented by high value services like CV typing, online application assistance, business registration and web and mobile app development.


Mission Statement

To empower our community by providing accessible, reliable and professional internet services from basic administrative tasks to advanced technological solutions.


Vision Statement

To be the leading internet services hub in my community, recognized for bridging the technology gap and inspiring growth for individuals and businesses.


Our Values

Accessibility | Reliability | Respect | Customer education
